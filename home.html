About Us

Balimbing is a barangay in the municipality of Boac, in the province of Marinduque,It has 7 purok.Site of Labanan sa Paye is the famous event ,July 31 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the entire Province of Marinduque to commemorate the victory of Filipino revolutionists in the Battle of Paye that took place on July 31, 1900.The site of encounter between Filipino Revolutionary Forces and American soldiers during the Filipino-American War.Our Baranggay is also known for having many fun events held every year
